Our Services

For Investors & Developers

Rental Property, Instant Interiors

Turn a vacant unit into a rental-ready property — fast. We handle everything: sourcing, procurement, and installation of all furnishings, fixtures, textiles, and art. You approve the concept; we deliver the finished space, ready for your first tenant.

Professionally designed and furnished properties command higher rents and shorter vacancy periods. A well-appointed unit attracts quality tenants and justifies premium pricing — directly improving your return.

We work with condo investors and developers across Miami who need a reliable, design-forward partner — not another variable to manage. Projects can be completed in as little as 6 weeks.

For Homeowners

Your Home, Designed

Whether you've just moved in or are ready to refresh your space, we bring the full design process to you — curating furnishings, textiles, fixtures, decor, and art that reflect how you actually live.

No construction. No overwhelm. Just a cohesive, finished space without the stress of doing it yourself.

Available for primary residences, second homes, and vacation properties throughout South Florida.

Who We Are

Design with precision.
Delivered.

Palm & Place is a Miami interior design studio. We handle furnishings, textiles, fixtures, and art — from concept to installation.

Our founder trained as an architectural designer and spent years managing large-scale construction projects. That background shows in how we work: every project has a clear scope, a real timeline, and someone accountable for seeing it through.

For investors and developers, that means units that lease faster and hold higher rents. For homeowners, a space that finally feels like yours — without spending months coordinating vendors yourself.

We work with condo investors, developers, and homeowners across Miami.
